Health care

If you become ill during your stay in the Republic of Belarus, contact your embassy or local consular department.

If you need emergency medical care, call 103. Emergency medical care is free.

If your case does not belong to the category of ambulances, then you can get paid planned medical care in clinics of Belarus.
